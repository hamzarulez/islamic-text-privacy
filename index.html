About Islamic Texts

Discover a comprehensive Islamic resource at your fingertips with Islamic Text.   • User-Friendly Design: Enjoy a clean, simple interface built for all ages and levels of knowledge.

Islamic Text is more than an app—it’s a tool to enrich your faith and knowledge. Whether you’re memorizing verses, researching Tafsir, or seeking inspiration from Hadith, we’ve got you covered. No distractions, just pure Islamic learning.

Privacy Policy

At Islamic Texts, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, Islamic Text ("the App").

Information We Collect

The App is designed to provide access to Islamic texts without requiring personal data. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., through optional feedback forms). Any data stored locally on your device, such as bookmarks or search history, remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

How We Use Your Information

We do not collect or use personal data for marketing, analytics, or third-party sharing. If you choose to send us feedback, we may use it to improve the App, but your identity will remain anonymous unless you specify otherwise.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties. The App does not include advertisements or tracking technologies that share data externally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available here. This policy was last updated on March 06, 2025.
